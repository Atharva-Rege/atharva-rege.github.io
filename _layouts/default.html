Atharva Atul Rege

I am a third-year undergraduate student at the National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK), Surathkal, pursuing a major in Computer Science and Engineering. I am a Research Intern at the I3D Lab, Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru, where I worked on developing diffusion-based methods for modifying the material properties of foreground objects, under the guidance of Prof. Pradipta Biswas.
